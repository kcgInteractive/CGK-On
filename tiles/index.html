The Man. The Myth. The Legend.

These books offer powerful antidotes to what is preventing the world from being a much better place: “The Upright Thinkers” by Leonard Mlodinow is a beautifully told story of the heroes, from Galileo to Darwin to Einstein, who transformed our lives by challenging the politically correct myths that hinder human progress. Our future depends on having such heroes. “Permissionless Innovation” by Adam Thierer reminds us that today’s technology has put people everywhere on the cusp of having better lives than anyone ever dreamed.
